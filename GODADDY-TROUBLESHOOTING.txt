📧 GoDaddy Email Account Verification Checklist
==============================================

❌ AUTHENTICATION REJECTED (535 Error)
This means your username/password credentials are incorrect or the email account isn't properly configured.

🔍 STEP 1: VERIFY EMAIL ACCOUNT EXISTS
-------------------------------------
1. Log into your GoDaddy account
2. Go to "My Products" → "Email & Office"
3. Find your alivio247.com domain
4. Check if these email accounts exist:
   - support@alivio247.com
   - info@alivio247.com

🔑 STEP 2: CHECK/RESET PASSWORD
------------------------------
1. In GoDaddy Email & Office dashboard
2. Click on the email account (support@alivio247.com)
3. Look for "Settings" or "Account Settings"
4. Find "Change Password" or "Reset Password"
5. Set a NEW password and remember it

⚙️ STEP 3: VERIFY SMTP ACCESS
----------------------------
1. In email account settings, look for:
   - "SMTP Settings" or "Outgoing Mail Settings"
   - "IMAP/SMTP Access" (make sure it's ENABLED)
   - "Less Secure Apps" (enable if option exists)
   - "App Passwords" (may need to generate one)

🧪 STEP 4: TEST CREDENTIALS
--------------------------
1. Update test-credentials.php with the correct password
2. Visit: http://yourdomain.com/test-credentials.php
3. Look for GREEN (✅) results

📝 STEP 5: UPDATE CONTACT FORM
-----------------------------
Once you find working credentials, update:
- contact-handler-smtp.php
- Line ~42: 'password' => 'YOUR_CORRECT_PASSWORD'

🔧 COMMON GODADDY ISSUES:
------------------------
- Email accounts must be created in GoDaddy first
- Password might be different from login password
- May need "App Password" instead of regular password
- SMTP might be disabled by default
- Some accounts require 2FA setup

💡 QUICK FIX ATTEMPT:
-------------------
Try these common GoDaddy passwords patterns:
- Same as your GoDaddy account password
- Same as webmail login password
- A password you set specifically for this email
- An "App Password" generated in settings

If nothing works, contact GoDaddy support and ask:
"I need to enable SMTP access for support@alivio247.com to send emails from my website"
